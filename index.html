Waiver

To be considered for employment by Unfinished Business, you must agree that:

  1. Unfinished Business may terminate your employment without notice or reason.
  2. The conditions of your employment; such as benefits, policies, and conditions are subject to change at the will of Unfinished Business.
  3. Unfinished Business is authorized to investigate all the content of this application and verify its validity.
  4. Unfinished Business has a zero drug and alcohol policy, and you give them permission to drug test you as a means of pre-employment screening.
  5. You may be asked to take a pre-employment physical to confirm you are capable of the required job-related tasks.
  6. You agree that Unfinished Business has the right to request from a consumer reporting agency an investigative consumer report. Such a report would include information relating to your credit score, character, general reputation, personal characteristics, and mode of living.
  7. You understand that your employment would begin in a state of probation. The probationary period will last 60 days, during which Unfinished Business retains the right to terminate your employment for any reason.
